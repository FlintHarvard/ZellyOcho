001 — About

The Origin

Denzel Young, better known as Zelly Ocho, is a rapper and recording artist from Cleveland, Ohio. Known for his chopper-like cadence and authentic autotune-trap sound, he's been grinding nonstop for nearly a decade.

He started rapping seriously at 18 in 2015 under the name Lil Ocho — a name his older friend Sam gave him based on his No. 8 jersey from his rec basketball team. He began cranking out remixes and perfecting his craft.

After two years, Lil Ocho evolved into Zelly Ocho and started dropping four to five projects annually. By 2020, his sound had become a movement that inspired a new wave of artists. He connected with producer 16yrold (Sig.Carlito) — the same producer behind Sheck Wes' massive hit "Mo Bamba" — and together they locked in on a string of projects.

His drug-induced early raps evolved into quick-paced cadences with cohesive bars about everyday struggles. Zelly proudly promotes the VLAD lifestyle — welcoming people of all backgrounds while channeling positive energy through his music.

"I know that I produce the best music when I'm not under the influence."

— Zelly Ocho

After enduring his father's absence and the influence of drugs and violence, Zelly chose to rise above his circumstances. In the words of Trippie Redd, Zelly Ocho is VLAD: "very loyal and determined."

003 — Philosophy

The VLAD Movement

V Violent
L Life
A Around
D Drugs

VLAD is more than an acronym — it's a lifestyle and movement that Zelly Ocho built from the ground up. It represents being present in the chaos without being consumed by it. Zelly is no longer on drugs, but he's still around them. That's the reality of where he comes from.



The movement welcomes people of all backgrounds. According to Trippie Redd, being VLAD also means being "Very Loyal And Determined" — a testament to Zelly's relentless work ethic and dedication to his craft and community.



Projects like OnlyVlad, Vlamily, VLAD 2, V2BM3, and his latest VLUTO, Vlada, Vlux drops all carry the VLAD banner — a brand that runs through everything Zelly creates.

006 — Journey

Timeline

2015
Starts rapping at age 18 as Lil Ocho. The name comes from his No. 8 jersey on his rec basketball team. Begins making remixes and grinding on SoundCloud.
2017
Evolves into Zelly Ocho. Starts dropping 4–5 projects per year. Builds a following through sheer volume and consistency on SoundCloud and Spotify.
2019
Drops It Could B Worse and Drugs in Hell. Connects with 16yrold (Sig.Carlito) — the producer behind "Mo Bamba." They begin a prolific creative partnership.
2020
Sound morphs into a movement. Releases Dungeon Music and Dead or Alive. His first pivotal project VLAD defines his new direction — "Violent Life Around Drugs."
2021
Connects with Trippie Redd via social media. Drops OnlyVlad and Vlamily. The VLAD lifestyle becomes a full brand and community.
2022
Signs with 1400 Entertainment. Launches the V2BM3 series ("Vlad To Be Me"). Tours internationally with Trippie Redd. Drops "SLYDE" with Sharkboy and "Duces" video (filmed in LA and Brazil). Viral tracks "Deadguy" and "No Wok" build cult following.
2023
Releases High Art and continues V2BM3 volumes. Member of the SIG collective alongside 16yrold. Steady grind — 70+ projects in the vault at this point.
2024
Biggest year yet — drops 10 projects in a single year. "V14D" with Trippie Redd on #2LV marks their first official collab. Drops H.O.E. EP and Dirty Drugs mixtape to close his "dirty drugs era."
2025
New era with DJ Phat — releasing VLUTO, Vlada, and Vlux. Also drops Wait on Vlad and Don't Talk to Me. The grind never stops.
